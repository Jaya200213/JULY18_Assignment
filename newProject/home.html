Technology

Technology is the application of knowledge for achieving practical goals in a reproducible way. The word technology can also mean the products resulting from such efforts, including both tangible tools such as utensils or machines, and intangible ones such as software

IT Services

IT services refers to the application of business and technical expertise to enable organizations in the creation, management and optimization of or access to information and business processes.The IT services market can be segmented by the type of skills that are employed to deliver the service (design, build, run).

Customer

A customer is an individual or business that purchases another company's goods or services. Customers are important because they drive revenues. Without them, businesses can neither survive nor thrive.
